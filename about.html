ABOUT SCARF

SCARF is an unregistered charity recently founded by Ellie Percey and Steve Gijrath in aid of refugees of war (Syrians, Afghans and Iraqis) who are having to leave their homes with very few belongings, journeying to Greece across dangerous waters where they arrive with even fewer belongings, before travelling across Europe to their final destination in freezing cold, winter conditions.

With the help of Amsterdam charity shop "De Boemerang" and a nearby storage depot, we are collecting donations of clothing and aid that we will sort, and send to places (camps, borders...) of need in Europe.

OUR STORY

At the end of last year we (Steve and Ellie) met while working as volunteers on Leros, a small Greek island (population approx. 8000). Our work benefitted hugely from the support of generous donations on our respective fundraising pages, as did refugees from Syria, Afghanistan, Iraq, and so on, for whom warm clothes, shoes and baby bottles were bought; so necessary for their journey across Europe.

Since returning from Leros we have visited Syrian friends in Germany who were quick to pull out their Lerian walking boots from under their beds, as a reminder of their gratitude. One thing that is constantly astounding about the majority of refugees we met on Leros is their graciousness and gratefulness when receiving aid – the help given to them is something they will never forget. Leros is seen by so many refugees as a very positive representation of what they think Europe can offer them: peace, stability and above all, new friendships and happiness.
